I am currently a member of the Personal Autonomous Robotics Lab (PeARL), where I am working on my honors thesis advised by Professor Scott Niekum. I am interested in developing data-efficient and generalizable algorithms for embodied agents, particularly with applications to robotic manipulation.

Previously, I was a member of the Texas Spacecraft Laboratory (TSL) and the team lead for Seeker, where we did research with NASA on visual navigation for satellites. We were advised by Professor Maruthi Akella. You can find all of our open-source code at github.com/autognc.
